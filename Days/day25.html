30 Days of Yoga-'DAY 25'


Day 25 of The 30 Days of Yoga journey! Dancing Warrior Yoga Sequence. Dance break! Don't decide where it ends! This day 25 yoga practice invites you to work on organic movement and creating a full body experience - that is true to you. No yoga robots! Stay receptive as you work to build strength and unlock space. Stretch, strengthen - work it out!
