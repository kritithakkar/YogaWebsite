30 Days of Yoga-'DAY 12'


Day 12 of The 30 Days of Yoga journey! Yoga For Spinal Health. This Day 12 practice promotes a happy and healthy support system. Seek balance as you stabilize the core musculature of the spine. Find a sense of ease as you strengthen the buttocks & legs and find support from within - that lasts. For real. Improve balance and coordination and ride the wave of yesterday's Shakti practice to create a focus that serves you. Keep coming back to your breath. Integrate the breath into each posture and every transition. Enjoy.
